Shoyu Ramen

Comforting soy-based noodle soup

Ingredients

- Ramen noodles
- Chicken or vegetable broth
- Soy sauce
- Garlic, ginger
- Spring onions
- Soft-boiled egg
- Bamboo shoots (optional)

Instructions

1. Simmer broth with garlic and ginger.
2. Add soy sauce to taste.
3. Cook ramen noodles separately.
4. Pour broth over noodles in a bowl.
5. Top with egg, spring onions, and bamboo shoots.
