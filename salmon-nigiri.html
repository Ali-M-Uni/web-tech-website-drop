Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Fresh salmon (sashimi grade)
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Wasabi (optional)

(Click to see steps)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the sushi rice with vinegar, sugar, and salt.
  2. Slice the salmon into thin pieces.
  3. Shape rice into small ovals and top each with a slice of salmon.
  4. Add a small amount of wasabi between rice and salmon if desired.
  5. Serve with soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi.
