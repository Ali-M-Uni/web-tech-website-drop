Mochi Ice Cream

A sweet and chewy Japanese dessert

Ingredients

- Glutinous rice flour
- Sugar
- Water
- Cornstarch
- Ice cream (any flavour)

Instructions

1. Mix rice flour, sugar, and water; microwave until dough forms.
2. Dust surface with cornstarch and roll dough out.
3. Cut into circles and wrap around small scoops of ice cream.
4. Freeze until firm and serve chilled.
